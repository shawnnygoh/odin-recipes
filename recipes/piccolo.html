Piccolo

Description

Piccolo is small coffee drink that is made with one part of espresso and two parts of steamed milk with a layer of silky foam on top

Ingredients

  • Coffee Bean Grinds - Espresso Grind
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Tamp your coffee grinds in a porter filter, ensuring a level flat surface.
  2. Extract a shot of espresso, between 20-30ml, with your filled porter filter and an espresso machine
  3. Steam your milk to around 60°C or 140°F, allowing enough air into it to create some microfoam, but ensuring that it’s stretched and silky.
  4. Pour between 40–60 ml of milk onto the espresso at a slight angle with a bit of height to integrate the espresso with the milk.
  5. Add a small layer of foam on the top.
  6. Voilà! Your Piccolo is complete.
