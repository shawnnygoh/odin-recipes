soba

Description

This dish is an awesome and easy to make Japanese noodle dish.

It's healthy, fun to make and fills the belly!

Ingredients

  • Zaru Soba
  • Soba Sauce
  • Ice
  • Spring Onions
  • Shredded Seaweed

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a pot until it starts to boil.
  2. Gently place the soba inside the boiling water and stir it constantly to ensure that it doesn't stick.
  3. Drain the water out after 7 minutes of cooking and pour the soba out into a bowl with ice.
  4. Wait for soba to cool down before serving on a plate with ice and topping it with spring onions, shredded seaweed and soba dipping sauce.
