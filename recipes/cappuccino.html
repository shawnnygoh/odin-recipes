Cappuccino

Description

Cappuccinos is an espresso-based coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with steamed milk including a layer of milk foam. Variations of the drink involve the use of cream instead of milk, using non-dairy milk substitutes and flavoring with cinnamon or cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • Espresso Shot
  • Milk
  • Cinnamon / Cocoa Powder

Steps

  1. Pull an espresso shot for your cappuccino.
  2. Steam your milk of choice.
  3. Pour in equal parts espresso, steamed milk and foam. (1/3 each)
  4. Add a bit of cinnamon or cocoa powder if you'd like.
  5. Enjoy!
