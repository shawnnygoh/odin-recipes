mac n cheese

Description

Cheesy, tangy.

You can't go wrong with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni in milk for 7 minutes.
  2. Add in some cheese, stir and mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture into a baking tray and leave it in the oven at 200 degrees celsius for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve with toasted breadcrumbs.
